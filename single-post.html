Volunteer With Us

14 April 2021

Have a passion for marine science that you want to share with your community? Why not volunteer with the Puget Aquarium! At the puget aquarium we have a youth program for those 18 years and younger and adult program for individuals over 18.

When you volunteer you are required to attend orientation and must to commit to at least 200 hours of volunteering.

We hope to see you join the team!
